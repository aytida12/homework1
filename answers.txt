Q: What is the difference between a merge and a rebase?

A: Merge lets you merge 2 different branches while rebase lets you merge the changes from one branch into another. Also, merge logs show the complete
history of merging while rebase logs show these logs linearly.

Q: What is a remote repository?

A: A remote repository is a version of your project that is hosted somewhere other than your local machine (Ex: the Internet or another network).
